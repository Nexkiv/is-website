Visit Duckburg

Welcome to Duckburg, the crown jewel of the state of Calisota! This bustling metropolis is a must-visit destination for any enthusiast or adventure seeker.

The illustrious history of our beloved Duckburg stretches back to the 16th century when the British explorer Sir Francis Drake established Fort Drake Borough on what is now known as Killmotor Hill. This strategic location served as a vital outpost for British interests in the region for over two centuries.

In 1818, a pivotal moment in our city's history occurred when the enterprising Cornelius Coot acquired the fort from its departing British occupants. Coot, demonstrating the ingenuity that would become a hallmark of Duckburg's citizens, renamed the settlement "Fort Duckburg" and transformed it into a thriving trading post. Under his stewardship, a small town began to flourish around the fort, attracting settlers and laying the foundation for our future metropolis.

However, it wasn't until the arrival of the legendary Scrooge McDuck in the early 20th century that Duckburg truly began its ascent to greatness. McDuck's decision to construct his iconic Mansion on Killmotor Hill in 1902 sparked an unprecedented economic boom. As Scrooge's wealth and influence grew, so did our fair city, rapidly expanding from a modest town into the bustling urban center we know today. By 1930, when Scrooge claimed the title of the world's richest duck, Duckburg had firmly established itself as a major metropolitan area and a global financial powerhouse. Today, Duckburg stands as a testament to the vision of Cornelius Coot, the business acumen of Scrooge McDuck, and the indomitable spirit of its citizens.

Industry and Innovation

Duckburg is at the forefront of technological advancement, boasting a thriving space exploration industry. Science and technology buffs will find plenty to marvel at, from cutting-edge laboratories to futuristic inventions that seem to pop up on every street corner.

Local Heroes

Gizmoduck

Breaking news, Duckburg! Our city's very own mechanical marvel, Gizmoduck, has once again proven why he's the talk of the town! This high-tech hero, with his arsenal of gadgets and gizmos, continues to astound citizens and confound criminals at every turn. From thwarting the notorious Beagle Boys to safeguarding Scrooge McDuck's legendary Money Bin, Gizmoduck's heroic exploits know no bounds. But it's not just his cutting-edge suit that makes him special - it's his heart of gold and unwavering dedication to justice that truly sets him apart. Whether he's zooming through the skies or rolling down our streets, Gizmoduck embodies the spirit of Duckburg: innovative, resilient, and always ready to lend a helping hand. So keep your eyes peeled, folks! Where there's danger, where there's trouble, you can bet your bottom dollar that Gizmoduck will be there, ready to save the day with a resounding "Blatherin' blatherskite!"

The Duck Avenger

Hold onto your feathers, Duckburg! The enigmatic Duck Avenger has struck again, leaving criminals quaking in their boots and citizens cheering for more! This masked mallard of mystery, known to some as Paperinik, continues to be the scourge of evildoers and the champion of justice in our fair city. With his high-tech gadgetry and unparalleled wit, the Duck Avenger outsmarts villains at every turn, always staying one webbed foot ahead of danger. From foiling alien invasions to unraveling complex conspiracies, there's no challenge too great for our caped crusader. But what truly sets the Duck Avenger apart is his uncanny ability to appear when least expected and vanish without a trace, leaving us all wondering: who is the duck behind the mask? One thing's for certain - whether he's swinging from rooftops or outsmarting nefarious plots, the Duck Avenger proves time and again that he's the hero Duckburg deserves. So sleep easy, fellow citizens, knowing that somewhere out there, our fearless feathered friend is watching over us, ready to swoop in and save the day!

Darkwing Duck

Attention, Duckburg! The terror that flaps in the night, the masked mallard of mayhem, the one and only Darkwing Duck has once again left his mark on our city's criminal underworld! This caped crusader, with his signature purple fedora and gas gun, continues to prove that he's not just the hero St. Canard deserves, but the one Duckburg needs! With a flair for the dramatic and a penchant for witty one-liners, Darkwing Duck turns every crime-fighting escapade into a spectacle of justice. Whether he's swinging from his Ratcatcher motorcycle or dropping from the Thunderquack jet, this daring duck detective always gets his villain. But it's not just his gadgets and gumption that make him a force to be reckoned with - it's his unwavering determination to protect the innocent and uphold the law. So, evildoers beware! When you hear the words "Let's get dangerous," you know that Darkwing Duck is on the case, ready to clean up the streets with a cloud of smoke and a hearty "I am the terror that flaps in the night!" Keep your eyes on the skies, Duckburg, for where there's trouble, there's bound to be a certain daring duck of mystery!
